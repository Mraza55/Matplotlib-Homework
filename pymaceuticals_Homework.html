Observations and Insights

1- There seems to be a strong correlation (.84)between mouse weight and tumor volume that could be a contributing factor to the effectiveness of any drug regimen. 2- There appears to be one potential outlier within the Infubinol regimen (see box plot below). While most mice showed tumor volume increase, there was one mouse that had a reduction in tumor growth in the study. 3-Capomulin had the most number of mice complete the study (See bar graph below).
